Ignite your storytelling prowess with the Plot Wizard Gamemaster's Narrative Design Workbook. Developed and introduced by veteran gamemaster and educator Shelia M. Cody, the Pentagon Plotting Model offers a comprehensive approach to crafting captivating narratives for tabletop roleplaying games (TTRPGs).

Within the Plot Wizard, you’ll discover thought-provoking and engaging guidelines, charts, diagrams, prompts, tips, and worksheets. These tools will help you outline dynamic and adaptable story arcs, craft plots that work together, and construct thrilling encounters for your players.

Whether you’re a newcomer or a seasoned tabletop campaigner, the Plot Wizard is an indispensable companion that will guide you into the realm of narrative mastery.

Your journey to unparalleled and unforgettable storytelling starts here.
